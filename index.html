SPACE:

DOES IT END?

STEPHEN HAWKING SAYS

We are just an advanced breed of monkeys on a minor planet of a very average star. But we can understand the Universe. That makes us something very special.
One of the basic rules of the universe is that nothing is perfect. Perfection simply doesn't exist... without imperfection, neither you nor I would exist.
Also, look up at the stars and not down at your feet. Try to make sense of what you see, and wonder about what makes the universe exist. Be curious.

NEIL ARMSTRONG SAYS

It suddenly struck me that that tiny pea, pretty and blue, was the Earth. I put up my thumb and shut one eye, and my thumb blotted out the planet Earth. I didn't feel like a giant. I felt very, very small.

As one of humanity's greatest explorers, my experiences and reflections hopefully continue to inspire generations to look beyond our own planet and strive for greater understanding of the cosmos.
